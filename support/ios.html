Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I get started with Clonio AI?

To get started, download Clonio AI from the App Store and follow the in-app instructions.

The app tells me I ran out of credits. What do I do?

If you've run out of credits, you can always purchase a subscription through the app. Subscriptions start at 5.99 USD/month and give you more accuracy as well as unlimited message credits. Alternatively, you can wait one day for your credits to restore.

How can I restore my purchases?

To restore purchases, go to the app's store page and tap on "Restore Missing Purchases." This will recover any previous purchases linked to your account.

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

If you encounter issues, try the following troubleshooting steps:

  • Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed.
  • Restart the app.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Contact support if the issue persists.
